Sponsor or Donate

The Stonehaven Pipe Band has been active since 1905, and in that time the band has had support from the local community to build its repuation, as a local and friendly Pipe Band.


If you wish to contact the band to become a sponsor or just to donate, then contact us using the contact form, or message us on Facebook.
