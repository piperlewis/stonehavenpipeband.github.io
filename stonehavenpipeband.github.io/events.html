Events

Specialising in many events from weddings, opening ceremonies, celebrating iconic events and all within the local community.


In the past decade alone, the Stonehaven Pipe Band has played at the Stonehaven Fireballs, the opening of the Open Air Pool, Rememberance services and much more.
