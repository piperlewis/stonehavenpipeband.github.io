About the Stonehaven Pipe Band

We are a small, local and experienced Pipe Band working within the heart of Stonehaven.

We provide the locals with a great sense of pride by playing Scotlandâ€™s traditional instrument. Attending Feein Markets which are a large variant of farmer markets with fun activities and live entertainment; Hogmanay Celebrations such as piping at the Stonehaven Fireballs and local pubs; and charitable events, we specialise for every occasion.


We are a Grade 4B band taking part in competitions for both full band and Quartets, allowing our band members to have the best experience possible.

Practices

As a band we have two practice sessions a week, covering all levels to ensure all members have an equal opportunity in practising their instrument. Our practices occur:


Monday: 19.30 - 21.00 for Drummers (Pads only), Practice Chanters and Beginners for all instruments.

Thursday: 20.00 - 21.00 for Bagpipers.


All practices are held at the Private Function Room at the Station Hotel, located next to Stonehaven train station. As tribute to the generosity of the Staff at the Station Hotel, Stonehaven Pipe Band perform every Hogmanay in the Private Function Room and in the bar to pay thanks for allowing the Pipe Band to grow in a Safe and Friendly environment.


If you wish to find out more about the Station Hotel then check out their Website or give them a like on Facebook!
